Our Right to Live

A student-led campaign advocacy promoting transparency, accountability, and active citizenship to defend human rights in the Philippines. We align with SDG 16 (Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions) and SDG 17 (Partnerships for the Goals).

  • Raise awareness about rights and accountability
  • Encourage active citizenship and civic participation
  • Build partnerships with schools and community groups
  • Collect feedback through polls & surveys

Why this matters

Without human rights, there can be no sustained peace, no stability, no protection from harm. No equality, no democracy, no space to speak up. No online safety, no end to the digital divide, no hope of an internet that puts people over profit. Without human rights, there would be no way to curb climate change, eradicate poverty, tackle racism, misogyny, homophobia or xenophobia. No way to protect the wellbeing and safety of children, young people, the elderly, disabled persons, refugees, or minorities. There can be no green and habitable future planet, no sustainable development, possibly no human future at all.

Our Advocacy

Our campaign aims to empower Filipinos through knowledge raising awareness by promoting transparency, accountability, and active citizenship to combat corruption and strengthen good governance. We encourage everyone to understand that real change begins when Filipinos unite. Our campaign fosters peace and fairness by bringing people together and equipping them with the courage and knowledge to speak out against injustice and advocate for what matters. Fairness is reflected in our right to speak up, and that right affirms our dignity and voice. It creates an environment where everyone feels safe, valued, and engaged in their roles. Fairness means freedom from bias, dishonesty, and injustice. Our campaign also strengthens partnerships and teamwork among Filipinos, because real change happens when we stand together with one purpose. By raising awareness, we help build trust in government institutions especially when they demonstrate accountability, transparency, and fairness. This trust can lead to stronger collaboration, a more resilient economy, and ultimately, national growth.

Campaign Focus

Our Campaign focuses on promoting transparency and accountability and active citizenship to combat corruption and strengthen good governance. It encourages the Filipino people to understand that real change begins when we stand together with one purpose --- to speak out, to listen, and to act for what is right. Just like the rally of the Trillion Peso March where organizations such as church groups, civil society organizations, student organizations,and labor unions protest responded to the alleged corruption in government flood control projects which was over P1.9 trillion spent over the past 15 years, more than half of which was allegedly lost to corruption, we gather as one to attain a common goal, it promotes the power of unity and collective voices in addressing corruption and to bring back the rights of the Filipino people, integrity, and hope in our nation’s governance that they would address the issue properly.

SMART Objectives

  1. Encourage Filipinos to understand that the real change happens/begins when we stand together with one purpose.
  2. To educate Filipinos by promoting transparency, accountability, and active citizenship to corruption, so that we can strengthen good governance.
  3. To promote peace, justice, and build stronger institutions.
